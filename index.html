( pronounced first name : sʊ-ʃɑ:ŋtʌ)


I am a Research Assistant in integrated Pattern Recognition and Biometrics (iPRoBe) Lab of Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Michigan State University (MSU). I work on voice biometrics and advised by Professor Arun Ross.

I was a member of Heterogeneous Learning and Reasoning (HLR) Lab at MSU. I completed my thesis under the supervision of Dr. Parisa Kordjamshidi and graduated from MSU with a Master's Degree in Computer Science.

Prior to this, I was a project engineer at Centre for Development of Advanced Computing, India. I primarily worked on creating Text to Speech systems and related technology solutions for Language Computing Group at CDAC, Mumbai.
